~ Music & Drawing ~

Instructions - Before You Begin

1.Take out a blank piece of paper and a set of colored pencils

2. Divide the paper into 3 sections and label each one with the title of each song in the playlist

3. Play each song - as you are listening, draw whatever shapes and colors come to mind

4. Keep in mind there isn't one correct answer

5. When you are finished, send a picture of your drawings to me via MS Teams or email
